Chapter 4: Troubleshooting

Even with the best tools, sometimes things don't go as planned. This chapter covers common issues and how to resolve them. Click to hear the overview.

Issue 1: Tool A appears greyed out. This usually happens if you haven't selected a compatible item first. Ensure your selection is correct.

Issue 2: The output looks distorted after applying Filter C. Check the filter mode and intensity settings. Often, resetting the filter and reapplying it with lower intensity helps.

If you encounter problems not listed here, try restarting the application or consulting the online documentation (link placeholder). You can also ask the assistant for help by right-clicking it.
